"Hey [name]. Glad you reached out — most people sit on a question like that for a week before they ask. Quick one before I tell you anything: what specifically pulled you to message me? Was it [specific thing in the short] or something else?"

One — you used their name. Not "hey friend" or "hey there." A name. The lead is a person and you're treating them like one.
